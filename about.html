About the store

Samuel Campbell founded The Cheap Collegiate in 1961, based on his experiences in college and with the mission of ensuring that all students can afford the textbooks and school supplies they need, regardless of income. Passed down through the generations, the store is now run by his grandchildren, Dean and Sam Winchester, who strive to provide only the best prices and quality customer service to the students in the area.

The Cheap Collegiate will match or beat any college-run bookstore price! If you see it cheaper in your own school's store, bring us proof of the sale price, and we'll either offer the same item for less or refund the difference.
